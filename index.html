About the Barangay

Serving the community of Sta. Lucia

Barangay Sta. Lucia is located in Novaliches, Quezon City, one of the most progressive communities in District 5. We are dedicated to providing efficient, transparent, and accessible public services to all our residents.

This digital portal is our commitment to modernizing how we serve you — bringing barangay services online, reducing wait times, and ensuring every resident has a voice.

How It Works

Get Started in 3 Easy Steps

Getting access to all barangay services online is quick and simple.

1

Get Your Barangay ID

Your Barangay ID Number is assigned by the admin. No registration needed your account is created for you.

2

Login with Your ID

Simply enter your Barangay ID Number on the Resident Login page to instantly access your personal dashboard.

3

Use Barangay Services

Borrow equipment, submit concerns, reserve the court, and stay updated with community events in real-time.
